Tips for Creating a Strong Password

  • Include a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters.
  • Add numbers and special characters.
  • Avoid using common words or phrases.
  • Make it at least 8 characters long.
  • Consider using a passphrase instead of a single word.

Why Password Strength Matters

Having a strong password is essential for protecting your accounts and personal information online.
Weak passwords are vulnerable to hacking and unauthorized access. By using a strong, unique password for each of your accounts,
you can greatly reduce the risk of security breaches and identity theft.
